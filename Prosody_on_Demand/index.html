1) Problem

  • Flat/neutral speech is weakly encoded; details are lost.
  • Daily impact: missed instructions, reduced independence.
  • Not only clinical: stress/noise/fatigue hurt recall for everyone.

2) Solution

  • Content‑equivalent real‑time transform: neutral → prosodic.
  • LLM‑guided prosody at phrase/sentence boundaries.
  • Low‑delay re‑synthesis for talks, briefings, calls, media.

3) Why It Works

  • Prosody gives timing cues that improve encoding + recall.
  • Everyday proof: rhymes & chants stick better.
  • Clinical evidence: prosodic/sung material often outperforms neutral.

4) Development Roadmap

  1. R&D Prosody Engine: streaming LLM
  2. Bench Latency, intelligibility, quality
  3. Hardware α Ear‑level prototype (embedded AI)
  4. Pilot Memory gains, usability, acceptability
  5. Verify Ethics, IP, standards pathway
