Technical Innovation

Unlike conventional hearing aids or passive tools, our system creates a closed-loop interaction between speech processing and brain activity, directly influencing memory encoding efficiency.

The AI-driven prosody engine analyzes incoming speech in real-time, identifying key phrases and transforming them into rhythmically structured formats that are easier for the brain to encode and recall.

Ear-EEG sensors provide continuous feedback about the user's cognitive state, allowing the system to personalize the prosodic transformations based on attention levels, stress, and individual learning patterns.
