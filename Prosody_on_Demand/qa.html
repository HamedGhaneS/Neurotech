Method is language‑agnostic; models are language‑specific. Start with English; extend later.

Safety, Ethics & Privacy

Are you recording conversations or brain data?
No default recording. Processing is ephemeral; ear‑EEG is pseudonymized and used solely for adaptation.
User control?
Clear toggle for prosody intensity and opt‑in for hooks/personalization.

Evaluation & Metrics

Primary outcomes?
Recall accuracy/speed, intelligibility/quality, subjective effort, and full latency budget.
Content‑equivalence checks?
Human raters + semantic similarity + key‑fact scoring.

Comparisons & Positioning

Different from hearing aids?
They amplify sound; we reorganize speech structure (rhythm/intonation/paraphrase) to support memory.
Different from note‑taking apps?
Those act after listening; we intervene during listening to reduce forgetting.

Roadmap, Risks, IP, Admin

Roadmap?
Prosody Engine R&D → Bench Prototype → Earplug α → Pilot Study → Verification & Standards.
Risks & mitigations?
Latency (distillation/edge), intelligibility (light‑prosody fallback), acceptance (style presets), regulatory (dual consumer/clinical pathways).
Funding alignment?
Strong fit for UKRI Proof of Concept / Blue‑Sky‑style calls (AI × Neurotech × Impact).
Partners?
Clinicians (Alzheimer’s/MCI), ed‑tech, neurotech startups, hearing‑aid/earbud manufacturers.
IP?
Content‑equivalent prosody transformation under tight latency + neuroadaptive loop.
Org & governance?
University ethics/IRB, consented data handling, institutional storage, procurement of ASR/TTS + custom ear‑EEG hardware.
